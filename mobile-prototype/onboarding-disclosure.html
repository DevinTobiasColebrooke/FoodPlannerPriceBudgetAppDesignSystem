A Quick Note on Data & Security

Welcome to Food Planner! To help you create the best meal plans and shopping lists, we'll ask for some information during onboarding, like:

  • Your dietary goals and preferences.
  • Number of people you're planning for.
  • Any food allergies or sensitivities.
  • Your approximate location (optional, for local store prices).

Our Promise:

  • We DO: Use your information to personalize your meal plans, estimate costs, and suggest relevant recipes.
  • We DO: Anonymize and aggregate data to improve our service for everyone (e.g., average food prices in a region).
  • We DON'T: Sell your personal, identifiable data to third parties.
  • We DON'T: Track your precise location beyond what you explicitly provide for store pricing (and you can opt-out or use a general region).

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. You can manage your data and preferences anytime in Settings.

By continuing, you acknowledge our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
