Oatmeal with Berries and Nuts

Main: Rolled Oats

Side: Mixed Berries, Almonds

Prep: 5 mins

Cook: 10 mins

Nutrition Stats (per serving) Estimates based on standard serving sizes. Source: USDA FoodData Central.

~350 kcal

10g

12g

55g

Serving Size:
1 Bowl (approx. 250g)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Rolled Oats
  • 1 cup Water or Milk
  • 1/2 cup Mixed Berries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 tbsp Chopped Almonds
  • 1 tsp Honey or Maple Syrup (optional)
  • Pinch of Cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Combine oats and water/milk in a small saucepan. Bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat and simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened.
  3. Stir in cinnamon and sweetener if using.
  4. Top with berries and almonds before serving.
