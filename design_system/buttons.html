4. Buttons

4.1. Base Button Style (`.btn-fp`)

  • Font: Poppins, Semibold or Bold (font-semibold or font-bold)
  • Padding: Mobile: py-2.5 px-5, Desktop: py-3 px-6. (Adjust for sizes)
  • Border Radius: rounded-lg (rounded-fp or 0.5rem) or rounded-full.
  • Transition: Smooth color and transform (e.g., transition-colors duration-150 ease-in-out).
  • Focus: Clear focus ring using focus:ring-fp-primary-green or focus:ring-fp-accent-blue and focus:ring-2 focus:ring-offset-2.

4.2. Primary Button (`.btn-fp-primary`)

Usage: "Start My Journey", "Save Meal Plan", "Confirm Budget".

4.3. Secondary Button (`.btn-fp-secondary`)

Usage: "Sign In", "View Details", "Clear Filters".

4.4. Text/Link Button (`.btn-fp-text`)

Usage: "Privacy Policy", "Skip", "Learn More".

4.5. Action/Eyebrow Buttons

Often smaller, contextual, may include icons.
